Buyer problem

A practical look at whether cable trays solve a real daily problem or just hide clutter.

What to check

  • Cable trays are useful when your desk has a back lip or pre-drilled mounting points.
  • The win is not aesthetics only: fewer dangling cables reduces accidental pulls when you move a chair or laptop dock.
  • Skip it if you frequently move desks, rent with strict no-screw rules, or use only two cables.

Verdict

Worth it for fixed desks with a monitor, dock, and charger. Skip for minimalist laptop-only setups.
