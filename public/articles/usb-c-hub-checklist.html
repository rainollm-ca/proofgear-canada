USB-C Hub Checklist Before You Buy

Buyer problem

The three specs that matter more than the product photo.

What to check

  • Check power delivery wattage; many cheap hubs pass data but underpower laptops.
  • Confirm monitor output: HDMI 4K at 60Hz is different from 4K at 30Hz.
  • Avoid unknown-brand hubs for permanent desk power unless certifications and return policy are clear.

Verdict

Buy known-brand hubs with clear specs. Skip listings with vague “4K support” language.
