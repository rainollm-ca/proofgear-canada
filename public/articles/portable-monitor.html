Buyer problem

Who benefits from a second screen and who should skip it.

What to check

  • Portable monitors help with charting, spreadsheets, coding, and reference documents.
  • They add cable clutter and battery drain.
  • Brightness and stand quality are often bigger issues than resolution.

Verdict

Worth it for multi-window work while travelling. Skip if you mostly answer email.
