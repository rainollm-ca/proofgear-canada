Buyer problem

What to check before buying a viral desk lamp.

What to check

  • Look for adjustable height/angle before RGB features.
  • Glare control matters more than maximum brightness.
  • Avoid health claims such as “prevents eye damage” unless backed by credible evidence.

Verdict

Buy for controllable task lighting. Skip if the lamp cannot be angled away from your eyes.
