A small desk accessory that is only useful in the right setup.

What to check

  • A stand helps if your phone is used for calls, timers, or two-factor authentication.
  • Charging speed and magnet strength vary widely.
  • It is clutter if your phone should stay away from your work surface.

Verdict

Buy for visible phone workflows. Skip if it encourages distraction.
