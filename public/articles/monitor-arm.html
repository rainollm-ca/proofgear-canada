The recurring complaints that matter before mounting a monitor.

What to check

  • Check monitor weight and VESA compatibility before style.
  • Cheap arms often sag with ultrawide or heavy displays.
  • Clamp depth matters on thick or glass desks.

Verdict

Buy only after checking weight, VESA, and desk edge. Skip unknown specs.
