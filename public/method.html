Review Method

ProofGear Canada uses a simple buyer-first rubric for desk and productivity gear.

Evidence tiers

  • Research-only: specs, seller checks, return policy, and repeated review patterns. We do not call this hands-on testing.
  • Quick test: product handled and basic function checked.
  • Full review: longer use, comparison, measurements, and follow-up notes.

Scoring priorities

  • Does the core job well.
  • Build quality and durability signals.
  • Value versus alternatives.
  • Ease of setup.
  • Returns, warranty, seller reliability.
  • Real user complaint patterns.
  • Safety, privacy, and claim risk.
  • Availability in Canada.

Editorial rule

Every positive recommendation must include a drawback or “skip if” section. We would rather publish a useful warning than a weak affiliate recommendation.

ProofGear Canada is an independent review project. We use clear disclosures and do not claim hands-on testing unless it happened. © 2026.
